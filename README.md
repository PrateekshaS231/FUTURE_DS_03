# FUTURE_DS_03
# 📊 Marketing Funnel Analysis

## 🔍 Project Overview
This project analyzes marketing campaign data to understand how users move through the funnel from leads to customers and identify key drop-off points.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze lead-to-customer conversion
- Identify drop-off rates in the funnel
- Compare performance across channels and segments
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- **Total Leads:** 41K  
- **Total Conversions:** 4.6K  
- **Conversion Rate:** 11.27%  
- **Drop-off Rate:** ~89%  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📱 Channel Performance
- Cellular campaigns convert **~3x better** than telephone  
- Strong preference for digital/mobile outreach  

---

### 👥 Customer Segments
- **Highest Conversion:** Students (31.4%), Retired (25.3%)  
- **Lowest Conversion:** Blue-collar (~6.9%)  

---

### 🎓 Education Impact
- University-educated customers show strong and reliable conversion  
- Lower education groups show weaker engagement  
- “Illiterate” category excluded from insights due to low sample size  

---

### 🚨 Funnel Drop-off
- ~89% of leads do not convert  
- Indicates major opportunity for optimization  

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Focus on high-performing channels (cellular)
- Target high-converting segments (students, retired)
- Improve messaging for low-performing segments
- Optimize follow-up strategy to reduce drop-offs

---

## 🛠️ Tools Used
- Python (Pandas)
- Power BI
